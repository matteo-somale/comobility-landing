### What Is Mobility Poverty?

In contemporary societies, often described as *hyper-mobile*, freedom of movement is widely considered a fundamental right. Yet, for many people, participation in social, political, and economic life is limited by insufficient access to mobility options. This condition is known as **mobility poverty**.
